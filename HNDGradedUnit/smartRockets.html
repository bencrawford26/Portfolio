Smart Rockets

Welcome to my version of Smart Rockets! The original app was developed by Jer Thorp and in that version each rocket had had thrusters randomly placed around its surface, giving it different directions of thrust. This version is much more simple in that there are no randomly placed thrusters so it lacks quality in comparison, however the rockets do still learn and adapt! The overall idea is quite simple, we have our group of rockets and some barriers for them to move past and hit the target at the top. As you can see, the rockets begin as mindless objects wandering aimlessly around the canvas. After a few generations of rockets they begin to get closer until one eventually hits the target. When it does the rockets have somewhat of an evolutionary leap and start to become more accurate.

I have also placed sliders around the canvas to allow you to change the size and position of each barrier. Feel free to play around with them and watch the rockets adapt! You can use the sliders to help them out through a kink, or you could use it against the rockets and try to make it as difficult as possible for them to reach the target. Whichever way you choose to use them I hope you enjoy watching these rockets evolve!
