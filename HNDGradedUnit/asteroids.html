Asteroids

Here we have a basic clone of the game Asteroids. My intentions were to apply the genetic algorithm to the asteroids to have them learn and evolve towards a higher chance of hitting the spaceship, but I was unable to finish it in time. For now, though it's just this plain boring version! Use W, A, S and D to move the spaceship and press the # key to shoot the asteroids. Stay tuned to this domain as I may be able to finish this app off one day!
